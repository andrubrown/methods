AB BioValley is a leading trinational biotech region in Europe covering the Alsace in France, Northwestern Switzerland and the region of South Baden in Germany. It is a unique location for highly successful developments in life sciences and biotechnology in Europe and an established network linking more than 40,000 people working in all areas of the life sciences and related industries. The goal of BioValley is to maintain a common culture, provide services, and enhance BioValley's profile in the whole world.
